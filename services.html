Our Services

At DD Plumbers our mission is simple, we want to remove the hassle and frustration traditionally associated with calling a local plumber. With a dedicated, experienced team of qualified plumbers and gas engineers, we will be there for you along every step of your journey and with our 24 hour emergency call-out service you can rest assured that when you need a plumber, we are already on our way. Emergencies (24 Hour)

Do you have an emergency? Call DD Plumbers at any time of day with and we will have an operative at your property within the hour [1]. Once the situation has been assessed, action will be taken immediately to resolve the problem. If parts or materials that we do not stock are required, the problem will be made safe temporarily and the job will be completed as soon as possible. This is usually the same day [2].

If any extra work, that is unrelated to the emergency, is required it can be carried out at our hourly rate or an estimate can be provided for larger jobs. We work cleanly and efficiently with minimal disruption, and we will always ensure that we leave your property clean and tidy following our visit. All work is provided with a 12 month guarantee and appointments are made at a time to you.

Please call our office on 01554 772744 to learn more about our hourly charges for emergency call outs. There are no hidden charges and we aim to make all pricing transparent before any work commences, so you can rest assured that when you need us we will be there for you.


[1] Emergency call out response times are location dependant.
[2] Emergency call out completion times are dependant on the availability of parts.
